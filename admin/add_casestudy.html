How to Add Article Content?

You have three options for adding content to your case studies:(Sort by what you added first)

  1. Add Title and Description:
    • Enter the article title in the designated field.
    • Enter the description of the title in the designated field.
    • After finishing, click the "Add" button.
    • You can repeat these steps if you want to add another title and description.
  2. Add an Image:
    • Use the "Add Image" option to upload the desired image.
  3. Word from the founder and CEO:
    • Enter the Word from the founder and CEO in the designated field.( Word From CEO ).
    • After finishing, click the "Add Word" button.
